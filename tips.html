Personality Tips

  • Have eye contact with your interviewer.

  • Dress code:- attend the interview by formal attire.

  • Don't wear new cloths.

  • Avoid strong fragrance.

  • Posture:- Don't put cross leg

Resume Tips

  • Personal info should be simple and elegant.

  • Follow standard resume format.

  • Provide your educational detail without mistake.

  • Use PDF format while applying jobs through online portal.

  • Always have extra copies of your resume in your hand.

  • Keep only important point about your projects in resume.

  • List out your skill set (Note: Make sure that the area in which you are expert).

  • List out your hobbies as well as.

Technical Interview Tips

  • Coding skills.

  • Problem-solving ability.

  • Testing ability.

  • Code reusability.

  • Adopting new programming languages easily.
